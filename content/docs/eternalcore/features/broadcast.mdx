## Introduction

The `/broadcast` command allows you to send messages to all players on your server.
This is useful for making announcements, sharing important information, or simply communicating with everyone at once.

<AlertBox type="tip" title="Tip">
  You can use color codes and MiniMessage formatting in your broadcast messages to make them more visually appealing.
  **Try out our [notification generator](https://www.eternalcode.pl/notification-generator)**
</AlertBox>

## Usage

```text
/broadcast [actionbar/title/subtitle/bossbar] [time] [-raw] <message>
```

<AlertBox type="warning" title="Note">
  If you do not specify the `[actionbar/title/subtitle/bossbar]` argument, the message will be sent as a standard chat message.
</AlertBox>
