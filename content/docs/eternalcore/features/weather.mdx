## Introduction

The `/rain`, `/thunder` and `/sun` commands allow you to change the weather conditions in different worlds on your server.

## Usage

```text
/sun [world]
/rain [world]
/thunder [world]
```

<AlertBox type="info" title="Note">
  - Without `[world]`, changes the weather in your current world.
  - Providing `[world]` changes the weather in that specified world.
</AlertBox>
